Ground Rules

Fair Play for Everyone

Simple rules that keep it fun, fair, and inspiring for all participants.

01

Mixed Teams Allowed

Teams can include students from any combination of Classes 7–10. Diversity makes stronger solutions.

02

All Skill Levels Welcome

No coding experience required. Ideas, design, and communication skills are equally valuable.

03

Respect the Clock

Stick to the schedule. Each session and presentation has a time limit — plan accordingly.

04

Originality Only

All ideas must be original. Your solution should be designed and built during the hackathon day.

05

Creativity Encouraged

Think outside the box. Wild, unconventional ideas are celebrated — not discouraged.

06

Be a Team Player

Collaboration is key. Support your teammates and respect other teams' work throughout the day.
