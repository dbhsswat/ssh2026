About the Event

Where Students Become Problem Solvers

Smart Student Hackathon 2026 is a one-day innovation challenge organized by Deep SWAT, held at Deep Boarding High School. Students from Classes 7–10 team up, think big, and build creative solutions that make student life better. No experience needed — just curiosity and teamwork.

Ground Rules

Fair Play for Everyone

Simple rules that keep it fun, fair, and inspiring for all participants.

01

Mixed Teams Allowed

Teams can include students from any combination of Classes 7–10.

02

All Skill Levels Welcome

No coding experience required. Ideas and communication skills are equally valuable.

03

Respect the Clock

Stick to the schedule. Each session and presentation has a time limit.

04

Originality Only

All ideas must be original and built during the hackathon day.

05

Creativity Encouraged

Think outside the box. Wild, unconventional ideas are celebrated.

06

Be a Team Player

Collaboration is key. Support your teammates and respect other teams.
